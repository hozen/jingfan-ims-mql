- Clearly articulate the additional value created by bundling

### 2. Flexible Implementation

### 3. Ongoing Optimization
- Regular review of pricing effectiveness
- Continuous customer feedback collection
- Competitive landscape monitoring
- Pricing model adjustment based on market response
